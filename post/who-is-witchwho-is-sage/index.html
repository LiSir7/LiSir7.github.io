《Who is Witch，Who is Sage？》

· 2023-03-12 ·

Witch and sage, two concepts we hold
Intriguing, mysterious, and wise, we're told
But who are they, these figures of old?
And what tales do their stories unfold?

A witch, a woman with magic to wield
A conjurer of spells, to make fate yield
She dances in moonlight, with secrets concealed
In tune with nature, her power revealed

A sage, a wise one with knowledge untold
A philosopher, thinker, and teacher, bold
With insights and wisdom, a story to unfold
A master of self, with a spirit to behold

But can these two ever be combined?
A witch who's also a sage, can we find?
A union of magic and wisdom entwined
A master of both, with a unique mind

So let us embrace these two concepts with care
And explore the stories that they each bear
For witch and sage, though different, they share
A power and wisdom beyond compare.

中文大意

风华绝代巫与智，
飘逸婉转人所嗟。
巫妖于魔法中妙，
智者于思想中悟。
月色寂静巫女舞，
繁花浓郁智人叹。
巫有幻境万物变，
智能洞悉万事理。
奇妙巫智何处寻，
拂石横琴隐林壑。
